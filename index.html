About Me

Originally from Bangalore, India I am a UX/Interaction Designer with a passion for what I call holistic design, which incorporates all facets of the problem space. I enjoy yoga, running ,backpacking and meeting new people. Currently I am completing my Masters in Human Computer Interaction at Indiana University while working as a research assistant in the field of wearable technology. Prior to that, I worked at Oracle Financial Servies Software and helped redesign enterprise banking software FLEXCUBE. My design process can be found here.
